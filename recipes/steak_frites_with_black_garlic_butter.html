Steak Frites with Black Garlic Butter

Steak Frites with Black Garlic Butter Recipe:

This recipe will instruct you how to cook a delicious steak frites with black garlic butter.

Marry Me Chicken Ingredients:

  • 1 head black garlic, peeled. Note: Roasted garlic may be substituted for black garlic
  • ¼ c. unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon brandy
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons kosher salt, divided, plus more for sprinkling
  • 2 pounds russet potatoes, spiralized into ¼-inch-thick strands
  • 1 tablespoon peanut or grapeseed oil, plus more for frying
  • 1 ½ pounds trimmed Charaolais hanger steaks or other grass-fed hanger steaks (2 to 3 steaks)
  • 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • Finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, for garnish (optional)
